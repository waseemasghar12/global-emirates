Case Studies

Representative engagements, told the way we actually work.

Logistics & Operations

A regional quick-commerce network losing margin to unmanaged delivery cost.

Problem

Cost-per-order had crept up 30% across three cities with no single owner of the number.

Thinking

Fleet mix, headcount and 3PL contracts were never benchmarked against each other.

Solution

A regional CPO benchmark model and a renegotiated vendor mix, installed as a system the team could run alone.

Impact

Cost-per-order reduced within a single quarter, with the tracking system still running after handover.

Retail & AI Commerce

A multi-branch retailer invisible to the AI agents now doing the shopping.

Problem

Legacy catalog and inventory systems couldn't be read by any AI shopping assistant.

Thinking

The gap wasn't demand — it was legibility. The global protocols already existed.

Solution

A catalog-normalization and protocol-integration layer, with fulfillment reliability as the differentiator.

Impact

First agent-routed orders live within one quarter, with a reusable integration for future protocols.

Family Office & Governance

A multi-household family with no visibility into its own AI footprint.

Problem

Four households, a dozen AI subscriptions, and no one auditing what any of them were actually doing.

Thinking

The family needed a governance layer, not another app.

Solution

A household agent inventory, a risk-scoring framework, and a standing advisor relationship.

Impact

A single monthly report replaced a dozen unmonitored subscriptions across the family group.
